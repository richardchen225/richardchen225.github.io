Ruiyang Chen

I am a master's student at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, advised by Prof. Heng Guo. Before that, I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science and Technology from Beijing University of Technology, Fan Gongxiu Honors College, in 2025, where I was advised by Prof. Wei Ma.

My research interests lie in 3D computer vision, with a current focus on feed-forward 3D reconstruction. I am also interested in world models and embodied intelligence, and I am always happy to discuss related ideas.
